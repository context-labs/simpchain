# Archive a Source

You have the option to archive or delete a source, thereby removing it from your source library.

<Callout type="info">
  If you have accidentally archived a source and wish to restore it, please
  reach out to Context support at hello@usecontext.io.
</Callout>

## How to Archive a Source

<Steps>

### Locate the Source

Select "Sources" from the left-side navigation to access your source library. Once inside, click on the source you intend to archive.

### Archive the Source

Next, click the "Settings" button located on the source navigation and scroll to the page's bottom. Here, you'll find the "Archive this Source" button. Clicking on this will trigger a confirmation modal. To proceed with the archiving, select "Confirm."

</Steps>
